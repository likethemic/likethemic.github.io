Frequently Asked Questions:

Which class is the right fit for my student?

It depends on the demeanor of your student. If your student prefers working in groups or participating in partner-based debates and is new to public speaking, then our Beginner Debate Group Class is probably the right fit for them. In addition, if your student has had previous experience debating and is looking for something more challenging, you should sign up for our Advanced Debate Group Class. If your student wants a more tailored and individualized experience, consider signing them up for the Individual Public Speaking and Debate Class (please contact us for inquiries regarding the individual class option)

What age range do you teach?

For group classes, I teach all kids between 3rd and 7th grade. However, if your child is younger, but you feel like they're ready to take on the challenge, then feel free to register. Before doing this, please make sure to contact me. If your child is above 7th grade, I only do individual classes. To schedule an individual class, contact me here.
