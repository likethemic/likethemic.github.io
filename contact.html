FAQ

Which class is the right fit for my student?

It depends on the demeanor of your student. If your student prefers working in groups or participating in partner-based debates and is new to public speaking, then one of our Beginner Debate Options is probably the right fit for them. In addition, if your student has had previous experience debating and is looking for something more challenging, you should sign up for one of our Advanced Debate Options.

What age range do you teach?

For group classes, I teach kids of all age ranges but usually pair students by skill level.
