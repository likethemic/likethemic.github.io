About the Founder

Sameer Paila is a rising freshman at New York University's Stern School of Business, with a passion for speech and debate, economics, and politics. He has over four years of experience debating on Northern California's Public Forum circuits. Noticing the polarizing climate that now defines American discourse, Sameer reached out to his community in hopes of teaching a more deliberate and respectful debate methodology to the youth. Moving forward, he wants to cultivate a more objective generation by reaching out to as many children within his community as possible.

Classes he teaches: Public Forum, Policy Debate

History

DebateGuru was founded in June 2018 with the intention of teaching students in the Tri-Valley area speech and debate. With what began as an organization with only a handful of students quickly expanded to the greater Bay Area and beyond. Currently, DebateGuru’s student base is expanding nationwide, with students from San Francisco to New York. Recently, DebateGuru has expanded its offerings to both online and in-person courses along with an increase in trusted and highly qualified staff members. In the future, we hope to continue to provide educational opportunities in various academic fields for students across the globe.
